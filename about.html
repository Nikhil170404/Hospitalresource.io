Who We Are

Hospital Resource Tracker is dedicated to providing real-time information about hospital resources to assist individuals in making informed healthcare decisions. Our team consists of passionate individuals from diverse backgrounds, including healthcare, technology, and data analytics.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve healthcare accessibility and emergency response efficiency by offering accurate and timely data on hospital bed availability, treatment equipment, and other critical resources. We believe that by providing this information, we can help save lives and contribute to healthier communities.

Our Vision

We envision a world where access to healthcare is equitable and efficient, regardless of geographic location or socioeconomic status. Through innovative technologies and collaborations with healthcare providers and organizations, we strive to make this vision a reality.
